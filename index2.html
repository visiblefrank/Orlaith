Orlaith Rafter is a Media, Communications & Performance specialist. She provides bespoke training & coaching solutions in the corporate world.

She is best known for her award-winning role of Robin Doyle in RTE’s flagship show Fair City. Orlaith is also one of Irelands top voice over artists, receiving a 2013 Radio Award and has voiced various high profile campaigns including TESCO, Galaxy Chocolate, Kerrygold, Superquinn and Electric Ireland as well as the lead continuity voice of RTE's Network 2. She was also the brand ambassador for Perlico, Fleetwood Paints, Sasha and Advanced Pitstop.

Orlaith has extensive media expertise in both TV and radio, contributing to top TV programming including The Late Late Show, The Afternoon Show, Tubridy Tonight, Seoige and O’Shea, The Den (RTE), IrelandAM (TV3) and hosted ‘24/7 Entertainment in the City’ on the City Channel. Radio broadcasting contributions include Newstalk FM, TodayFM & FM104.

She is an honours graduate in Public Relations (PRII), Performance (Gaiety School of Acting) and Vocal Technique (Bristol Old Vic, Patsy Rodenberg, Kevin Crawford) with over 17 years of expertise in broadcasting, performance, voice, modelling, producing, directing, writing and workshop facilitation.

In 2011 she co-founded Boil In The Bag productions, who specialized in presenting Shakespearean texts in modern contexts to engage teenage audiences with the 400 yr old text in new forms, facilitated through workshops, roleplay, improvisation & devised scenarios.

What We Do

Corporate Stage is a unique Training and Coaching methodology which teaches INDIVIDUALS and GROUPS how to PRESENT their very best selves with maximum IMPACT. We provides bespoke Training and Coaching solutions to communicate Confidence, Clarity and Conviction for our clients on the Corporate Stage.We specialise in helping clients to be great Communicators by drawing on a range of PERFORMANCE and VOCAL disciplines to help Professionals project a Confident, Clear and above all Polished image.

COMMUNICATION is what others receive…….. NOT necessarily what you say, or do.

REMEMBER:
55% is VISUAL
38% is TONAL
7% is VERBAL

VOCAL DELIVERY can be broken down into PACE, PITCH, VOLUME, BREATH and PAUSE. All of these allow for greater CLARITY. And all of the above can be enhanced by BODY LANGUAGE.

“I don’t want to train you to be a near perfect Orator who can hypnotise a room. I want you to develop PRESENCE. Presence to inspire others, to communicate with Clarity, Confidence and Conviction. To bring your very best self to the table. These skills can be taught! This isn’t just about Presentations skills. This is about using Performance Techniques for everyday life within the business community and ultimately Presenting your very best self. ”

Orlaith Rafter.
